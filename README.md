# Ecommerce Sales Dashboard

## Overview
This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze ecommerce sales performance across different states, categories, customers, and payment methods. The dashboard provides insights into revenue, profit, quantity sold, customer behavior, and monthly profit trends to support business decision-making.

The project focuses on transforming raw ecommerce sales data into meaningful visual insights using Power BI.

---

## Business Problem
The objective of this project was to answer key business questions such as:

- Which states generate the highest sales revenue?
- Which product categories contribute the most sales?
- Which sub-categories generate the highest profit?
- How does monthly profit fluctuate throughout the year?
- Which payment methods are most commonly used?
- Who are the top customers based on purchase amount?

---

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- Total Sales Amount
- Total Profit
- Total Quantity Sold
- Average Order Value (AOV)

### Interactive Filters
- Quarter slicer
- State slicer

### Visualizations
- Sales by State
- Quantity by Category
- Profit by Month
- Sales by Customer
- Quantity by Payment Mode
- Profit by Sub-Category

---

## Key Insights
- Maharashtra generated the highest sales among all states.
- Clothing accounted for the largest share of product quantity sold.
- COD was the most preferred payment method among customers.
- Printers generated the highest profit among all sub-categories.
- Monthly profits fluctuated significantly, with strong performance in January and November.
